Rooted in Purpose: An Interview with Rachel Domb

By Ella Faith Albano · February 17, 2025

Five years ago, Rachel Domb created an eco-friendly and plant based snack company called Rooted Living. Her passion for sustainability and healthy eating has motivated her to pursue this venture. As a Northeastern alumna, she was able to use the support of WISE and Sherman Center to expand Rooted Living. Which has grown into a company that Domb is now running full-time.

Domb has been recognized for her achievements by Northeastern, Women Who Empower, BostInno, and more. Rooted Living is being sold around campus in Wollaston's and dining halls. The team is working on exciting new developments that should be in action by the end of this year.

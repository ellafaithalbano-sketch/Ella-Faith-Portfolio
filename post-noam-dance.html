Staying in Motion: Noam's Passion for Dance

By Ella Faith Albano · March 18, 2025

Noam Dor, the current Studio Administration and Operation Co-op for Boston Ballet School, has been dancing for most of his life. At the age of 3, his mom put him into dance classes and his passion blossomed from there. He continued to dance through high school and still continues to participate in different dance clubs on campus.

Next semester, Dor will be the president of Northeastern's no auditions and no cuts dance company, No Limits Dance Crew, which he is already choreographing for this semester. He also dances with Northeastern University Dance Company, another dance company on campus.

While at his time at Boston Ballet School, Dor has gained more insight into the programs and operations that the school has to offer. He hopes to stay in the arts industry in some capacity, wanting to keep dance in his future. Dance holds a special place in his heart and encourages all to try and experience the art form.
